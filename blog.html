Joshua's Blog Post 25th Oct 2016

The Power of Progressive Web Apps

Key benefits of PWAs
PWAs leverage service workers to cache assets, ensuring lightning-fast load times even on slow connections.
PWAs can work offline, providing a consistent user experience even when there's no internet connectivity.
Engage users with timely notifications, driving app re-engagement and boosting user retention.
PWAs offer a native app-like feel with features like full-screen mode and home screen shortcuts.
By embracing PWAs, businesses can deliver exceptional user experiences, improve conversion rates, and boost customer loyalty.
